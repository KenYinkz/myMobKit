Tips and Tricks - Surveillance


Send motion detected images to Gmail

  1. Configure a valid Gmail account and its password. You can create a separate Gmail account for the purpose of myMobKit notification.
  2. With a valid Gmail configured, select Notify by email check box to enable email notification. You can also select Email storage to attach the image in the email.

Upload images to Google Drive

  1. Select Google Drive storage and you will be asked to authenticate yourself to allow myMobKit to access Google Drive.
  2. The images will be saved into a folder named after your device unique id.

Disguise camera

  1. Select Disguise camera to have a web browser overlaid on your camera view.
